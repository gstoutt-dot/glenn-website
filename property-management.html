Sundance

From company-building to managing serious scale.

Glenn founded and built Sundance Property Management Corp. into one of the largest privately owned property management companies in South Florida. Under his leadership, the portfolio exceeded $500 million in assets.

That required more than operations. It required trust from boards, strong systems, steady client relationships, and the ability to lead both people and properties with consistency.

Hurricane Andrew

Leadership tested under real pressure.

Following Hurricane Andrew, Glenn coordinated the recovery of more than $250 million in property damage across managed assets. That work demanded fast decisions, communication under pressure, vendor leadership, and a calm command structure when communities needed direction most.

It remains a defining example of what high-level property leadership looks like when the stakes are real.
