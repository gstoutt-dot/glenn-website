Legacy in Action

At 25, Glenn was awarded a major municipal contract few believed someone his age could win. That moment became the foundation of a career built on discipline, execution, and long-term thinking.

Over the decades that followed, he developed and managed projects ranging from residential communities to large-scale association environments, always with a focus on quality, accountability, and lasting value.

His experience in aviation reinforced a mindset of precision, preparation, and responsibility — principles that now define how Stoutt Property Management operates today.

Every property, every board, and every decision is approached with one standard in mind: build something that lasts.
