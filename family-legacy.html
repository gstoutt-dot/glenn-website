A Personal Chapter

Caregiving changed the meaning of leadership.

Glenn spent over a decade as the primary caregiver for his late wife, Lisa Stoutt. That experience deepened his patience, empathy, and understanding of what service really means. It transformed leadership from something professional into something personal.

This chapter is inseparable from the values behind Stoutt Property Management today.

Legacy Through KDA

Purpose carried forward through service.

Together, Glenn and Lisa shared a deep passion for helping others through the Keeping Dreams Alive Foundation. That mission continues through stewardship, mentorship, and a commitment to changing lives in at-risk communities.

It is why family, purpose, and legacy belong in the brand story — because they explain the heart behind it.
